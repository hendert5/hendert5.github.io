Our Capabilities

At the Space Technolgoies Laboratory we count with different capabilities to make your space mission a reality. We offer a complete set of services for design, test, validation and verification, and clean room assembly.

To design we count with multiple tools that allows to be flexible all the way from CATIA and Solidworks, to STK, Ansys, and more. Once the product is more matured we have the capability of 3D printing in resin or FDM to evaluate these designs.

For space qualification, validation, verification, and assembly we have the following equipment and spaces:
* Electrical bench with different tools to assemble and test electronic circuits from regular components to surface mount.
* Optical bench with calibrated mounting points to perform camera calibration and validate optics.
* 1 cubic meter Thermal Vacuum Chamber that is able to reach 10^(-7) Torr and a temperature range of -112F to 392F (-80C to 200C).
* Vibration table and shaker for up to 300kg, perfect for testing small to medium satellites.
* Class 10000 clean room that can be arranged based on the custmer needs.

For other experimentation such as rendevouz, robotics, computer vision, and more we also have a Sawyer Robot with 7-DOF and multiple computers. 

The lab also offers the service to write software for different space tasks from camera calibration for star trackers, to machine learning applications in space such as objected detection and classification.

Additionally, we also have 2 telescopes (11” and 14”) and a ground station with antennas that we use for space awareness research, including tracking of satellites in different orbits, launch vehicle tracking, and more.

If you have a specific need, don’t hesite to contact us so we can discuss how can we fullfill your needs.
